What is Unemployment?

Unemployment occurs when people who are willing and able to work cannot find jobs. It is one of the most important indicators of a country's economic performance and social well-being.

Main Reasons

📉 Economic Recession
Business slowdowns reduce hiring
🤖 Automation
Jobs replaced by technology
🎓 Lack of Skills
Mismatch between skills & jobs
📈 Population Growth
More workers than jobs
🌍 Globalization
Jobs moving offshore
🏭 Seasonal Jobs
Temporary employment cycles

💡 Solutions to Unemployment

🎓 Skill Development
Vocational training & upskilling programs
🚀 Entrepreneurship
Startup funding & business incubators
🌱 Green Jobs
Renewable energy & sustainable projects
💻 Digital Economy
Freelancing & remote work platforms
🏭 Manufacturing Boost
Local industry development
👥 Gig Economy
Flexible short-term job opportunities
